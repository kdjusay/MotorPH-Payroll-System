Introduction

1. Introduction

Welcome to the User Guide for the MotorPH Payroll System! This section serves as an introductory overview of our comprehensive payroll management solution. Whether you're an employee, a member of the HR department, part of the finance team, or an IT professional, this guide will help you navigate and utilize the system effectively to streamline payroll processes and enhance organizational efficiency.

1.1. User Roles

Employee:

- Log in to the system securely.

- Submit leave and overtime requests.

- View and download payslips.

HR Department:

- Approve or reject leave and overtime requests.

- Access and update employee information.

- View and download payslip history for employees.

Finance Department:

- Process payroll for employees.

- Generate payroll reports for financial analysis and auditing purposes.

IT Department:

- Process payroll for employees.

- Access login audit reports to monitor system usage, employees' entry time, and security.
