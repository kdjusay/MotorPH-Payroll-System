Introduction

1. Introduction

Welcome to the User Guide for the MotorPH Payroll System! This section serves as an introductory overview of our comprehensive payroll management solution. Whether you're an employee, a member of the HR department, part of the finance team, or an IT professional, this guide will help you navigate and utilize the system effectively to streamline payroll processes and enhance organizational efficiency.

Employee:
